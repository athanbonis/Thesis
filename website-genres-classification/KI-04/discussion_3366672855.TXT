Tatiana Mikhailovna Velikanova passed away.

On 19 September on the 71st life year Tatiana Mikhailovna Velikanova passed away. Tatiana Mikhailovna was one of the most respected and authoritative activists of the Soviet human rights movement. Her personality influenced to a great extent the moral and intellectual level of the Russian human rights movement.
